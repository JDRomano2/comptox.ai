Representation of an Adverse Outcome Pathway (AOP)¶

class comptox_ai.aop.AdverseOutcomePathway(name)¶

Adverse Outcome Pathway—A framework for conceptualizing how toxic exposures result in downstream adverse phenotypic effects, especially in humans.

AOPs basically constitute the “pinnacle” of toxicological knowledge, and are therefore one of the central components to the Comptox Ontology. Most of the algorithms and models applied to the data seek to either discover new AOPs or validate existing/proposed AOPs.
